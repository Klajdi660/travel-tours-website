FlyAlbania Accomodation

Epirus Hotel

Enjoying a beachfront location, the elegant Epirus Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms and studios with cable TV, free Wi-Fi and free parking. The hotel also has 2 beach bars and a restaurant.
You can relax on the terrace shaded by straw umbrellas, while the hotel restaurant serves samples of traditional Albanian and Italian cuisine.
The local bus station is about 500 m away, while the town centre featuring the Sarandë Museum is 1 km from the hotel.

Mucobega Hotel

Offering a private pebbly beach with free sun loungers and parasols Hotel Mucobega is located in Sarandë and provides air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony in each unit. Water sports facilities and a beach bar are also provided, while free WiFi access is available in all areas.
Rooms here come with a flat-screen cable TV, a desk and a fridge. The private bathrooms are fitted with a shower and a hairdryer, and also provide free toiletries. A sea view can be enjoyed from all rooms.

Hotel Butrinti

Located in Sarandë, 100 m from Saranda City Beach, Hotel Butrinti & SPA provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. With a bar, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. The accommodation offers room service, luggage storage space and currency exchange for guests.

Demi Hotel

Offering a restaurant and a private beach area, Demi Hotel is located in Sarandë. Free WiFi access is available. At Demi Hotel you will find a bar and shared lounge.
Each air-conditioned room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV with satellite and cable channels, a minibar, a seating area and a balcony with sea and city views. Featuring a shower, private bathrooms also come with a hairdryer and free toiletries.
The hotel offers free parking. This is our guests’ favourite part of Sarandë, according to independent reviews.

Agimi Hotel

Featuring swimming pool with free sun loungers and parasols, Hotel Agimi also offers air-conditioned rooms with a furnished balcony and LCD satellite TV. An on-site bar and restaurant, serving Mediterranean and Albanian cuisine are set on a large terrace overlooking the sea.
Each room features a minibar, a desk and a bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Free WiFi is also provided while free public parking is available on site. A souvenir shop and a grocery shop are available in the hotel.

Glow Boutique Hotel

Located in Sarandë, next to the Flamingo Beach and Mango Beach, Glow Boutique Hotel & Suites offers modernly styled rooms and suites. The hotel comes with an outdoor swimming pool and sun terrace featuring sun loungers.
Stylish and comfortable rooms come with a desk and a flat-screen TV. Private bathrooms feature a walk-in shower and luxury toiletries. Suites also offer a seating area with a sofa. Some units also come with a balcony and outdoor furniture.

Buzë Hotel

Located in Sarandë, less than 1 km from Flamingo Beach, Buzë Boutique Hotel provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a seasonal outdoor swimming pool and a bar. This 4-star hotel offers a private beach area and a concierge service.
The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.At the hotel each room includes a wardrobe and a private bathroom. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Buzë Hotel. The accommodation offers a terrace.

Jaroal Hotel

Featuring its own private beach, a restaurant and a bar with terrace, Hotel Jaroal has air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, LCD satellite TV and balcony in all its rooms and suites. Town centre is 700 m away. Free private parking is provided on site.
Minibar and carpeted floors are standard facilities in all units. The private bathroom comes with shower, hairdryer, bidet and free toiletries. Jaroal Hotel is just a 3-minute walk from a green market and the nearest grocery shop. Main bus and train stations are 1.5 km from the hotel.

Harmony Hotel

Featuring an elegant restaurant with views of Sarandë, the newly-built Harmony Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi just steps away from the beach area. It is located near the SH81 State Road connecting Sarandë with the ancient archaeological site of Butrint.
Including some handmade furniture and LCD satellite TV, all rooms come with a minibar, private bathroom with bath, and a balcony. Some rooms offer views of the Corfu Island, some have sea view and some overlook the Sarandë City.

Nertili Hotel

Enjoying a seafront position and offering a private beach area with free sun decks and parasols, Hotel Nertili is also provides a bar and a restaurant with terrace boasting sea views. Its rooms are fitted with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. The centre of Sarandë is about 1 km away.
All rooms feature a balcony with seating area and sea views. They comprise a cable TV and a bathroom with shower. Laundry and ironing service is possible at a surcharge.

Apollon Hotel

Benefiting from a private beach with free sun loungers and parasols, Hotel Apollon offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, sea-view balcony and satellite TV. Free private parking is provided, and local buses with links to Sarandë beaches stop right by the hotel.
Each room has a minibar and a bathroom with hairdryer, slippers and free toiletries. Guests can relax at the bar, which has a terrace. Breakfast is served at the hotel restaurant. Massages can be enjoyed at the beach during high season.

Da Luz Boutique Hotel

Located in Sarandë, 70 m from Sarande Main Beach, Da Luz Boutique Hotel provides accommodation with a bar, free private parking and a garden. The accommodation features room service and free WiFi throughout the property.
A à la carte breakfast is available daily at the hotel. Saranda City Beach is 1.1 km from Da Luz Boutique Hotel, while Maestral Beach is 1.4 km away. This is our guests’ favourite part of Sarandë, according to independent reviews.

Murati Apartments

Located in Sarandë, 3 km from Flamingo Beach, Apartments Murati provides accommodation with free WiFi, air conditioning and access to a garden. Each unit is equipped with a balcony, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a seating area with a sofa, a flat-screen TV, a washing machine, and a private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer.
A stovetop and kettle are also provided. The apartment provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. Hiking can be enjoyed nearby.

Elti Apartment

Located in Sarandë, Elti Apartment features accommodation with a flat-screen TV. Complimentary WiFi and Netflix is provided. Every unit features air-conditioned rooms and a kitchen with a fridge and kettle. Some units feature a terrace and/or a balcony with sea or mountain views.

Sea Horizon Vsl

Sea Horizon Vs Suites & Apartments has sea view, free WiFi and free private parking, situated in Sarandë, 2.1 km from Flamingo Beach.
All units here are air-conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV, a living room with a sofa, a well-equipped kitchenette and a private bathroom with bidet, a hairdryer and free toiletries. A fridge and toaster are also available, as well as a coffee machine.
